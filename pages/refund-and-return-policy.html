Refund and Return Policy

At Almeria Mobile, we want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not satisfied with your order, we offer a hassle-free return and refund policy.

Return Eligibility

  • Returns are accepted within 7 days of delivery
  • Product must be unused, unwashed, and in original packaging
  • Product must be in resalable condition with all tags attached
  • Return request must be initiated by contacting us within 7 days of delivery

Non-Returnable Items

  • Products purchased during sale or clearance
  • Customized or personalized products
  • Used or damaged products
  • Products without original tags or packaging

Return Process

  1. Contact us on WhatsApp at +91 98243-45824 or email at inquiry@almeriamobile.in within 7 days of delivery
  2. Provide your order ID and contact information
  3. Ship the product back to the address provided by our team
  4. Once we receive your return, we will do a quality check on the product
  5. If the product passes our quality check, we will credit the amount to your bank account in 5-9 working days

Refund Policy

Please mention your order ID and contact information on the package. Once we have received your return, we will do a quality check on the product at our end, and if the product passes our quality check, we will credit the amount we paid to your bank account in 5-9 working days.

Cancellation Policy

We aim to process and dispatch orders as quickly as possible. However, if you wish to cancel your order, please contact us as soon as possible.

If the order has not been dispatched, we will cancel it and process a full refund.

Important Notes

  • Processed by us for the same customer because we bear the loss of two-way packing and courier costs, so in that case we have the complete right to cancel such an order.
  • If customer place a Prepaid order and still refuses to accept the order at the delivery time, it gets returned to us without accepting the delivery and the same customer will place an order again. Then we will decide whether to process and dispatch their order or cancel their order.
  • As per the company's guidelines, a charge of ₹250 per order may apply to cover returns or refunds to the customer's bank account as handling expenses. (₹250 Counted as Two ways Courier Cost, Packing Cost and Bank Charges)
  • As per the company's guidelines, to bear the cost or expenses of material, labor charges, and packaging used in making the product, the above amount shall be recovered from the customer.
